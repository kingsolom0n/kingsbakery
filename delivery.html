COVID-19 Update

The Corona virus has presented a lot of challenges especially to the food service business. Please be assured we are taking all precautions recommended by the CDC and other health organizations. Our workers wear gloves and masks at all times while preparing the food and also while delivering it.

Delivery Options

We deliver for a flat rate of $10 within 10 miles of our local area. Stay tuned, we are working towards expanding our delivery area!

We two delivery options: standard and gift

Standard - We deliver the items in our standard packaging. Packaging is a recyclable paper box.

Gift - Your items comes in our gift packaging ready to give out. No physical receipt is included in the package.

Delivery Hours

Our current delivery hours are Monday through Friday, from 11 AM to 6 PM.
