The King's Bakery

We are a bakery shop offering many delicious treats right in the heart of Downtown Decatur. We offer everything to satisfy your sweet tooth! Options include milkshakes, assorted varieties of cupcakes, cheesecakes, and so many more!

We pride ourselves on using the highest quality and freshest ingredients available. Everything is made fresh daily. Come check out our seasonal options!

Variety

In the mood for something sweet, but not sure what you want? We have a very selection of sweets to choose from and our workers can provide options on new sweets to try out!
