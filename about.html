About Us

The King's Bakery was founded in 2010 by Solomon Otoo to bring delectable goods to the people of Decatur and the surrounding regions. Since it's inception, The King's Bakery has won many awards. However nothing comes close to the smiles we see in our store from our satisfied customers.

Community Outreach

Our community gives us so much and we like to contribute back however we can. We have our Thanksgiving and Christmas food drives where we donate time and money to families in need. In addition, we also volunteer at senior homes and provide desserts to them every other weekend. We have a specialty item every month and 50% of the proceeds go towards helping the homeless people in our community.

If you believe in helping in the community and would like to join us in our volunteering efforts, please reach out to us at info@thekingsbakery.com! We're always in need of more help.
